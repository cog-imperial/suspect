Directed Acyclic Graph¶

class suspect.dag.ProblemDag(name=None)¶

The optimization problem represented as Directed Acyclic Graph (DAG).

The vertices in the DAG are sorted by depth, defined as

\[\begin{split}d(v) = \begin{cases} 0 & \text{if } v \text{ is a variable or constant}\\ \max\{d(u) | u \in c(v)\} & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}\end{split}\]

where \(c(v)\) are the children of vertex \(v\).

Attributes

name (str) the problem name.
variables (dict) the problem variables.
constraints (dict) the problem constraints.
objectives (dict) the problem objectives.
vertices (iter) an iterator over the vertices sorted by \(d(v)\).

depth¶

The depth of the expression.

The depth of the expression is defined as 0 if the expression is a source (Variables and Constants), otherwise it is the maximum depth of its children plus 1.
